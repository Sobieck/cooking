2 lbs kielbasa, sliced 1/4-inch thick on the bias

veg oil

16 cloves garlic, minced

2 lbs. red kidney beans, soaked overnight in brine

4 quarts chicken broth

2 lbs red bliss potatoes, cut into 1/2 inch cubes

12 ounces fresh kale, approximately 4 handfuls

0.5 cup red wine vinegar

1 tsp freshly ground black pepper


# cook

Sauté the kielbasa until well browned and fat rendered, medium low heat. 

15 minutes, remove kielbasa. 

Sauté the garlic for 1 - 2 minutes, stirring. 

Add liquid and red kidney beans

Bring up to simmer, put on cover. Put in Oven

Cook for 1.5 hours

Add potatoes

Cook for 45 minutes

Add kale, cook for 15 minutes

Add Red wine vinegar and black pepper. Add Kielbasa
