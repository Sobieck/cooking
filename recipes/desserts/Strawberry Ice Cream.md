330 granulated sugar

    188 g sugar

    142 g sugar

900 g (~2 lbs) frozen strawberries

980 g (4 cups or 32 fl oz) whole milk

950 g (4 cups or 32 fl oz) heavy cream

1 tablespoons vanilla paste 

300 g (20) large egg yokes

2 g salt

225 g frozen sliced strawberries

    112 g per batch

# Steps

Heat frozen strawberries and 188 g sugar until strawberries are broken down.

Add milk and vanilla over medium-high until just below a simmer. 175 degrees F

Meanwhile whisk 142 g sugar with yolks until slightly thickened and whisk leaves a trail. 

Whisk remaining sugar and eggs, temper with entire mixture. use 3 quart stainless

Use strainer to return mixture to clean pan (blue cast iron)

Over med heat, stir while scrapping bottom. 

It is done when it coats spoon and when steaming. egg yorks done at 180 F 

Strain into bowl over ice, add salt. Stir from time to time until cool. 

Refrigerate over night. Make ice-cream next day. 

Add sliced fresh strawberries while mixing

Make ice cream in 4-5 cup batches
