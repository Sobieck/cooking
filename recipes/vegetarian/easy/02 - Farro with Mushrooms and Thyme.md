340 g whole farro 

36 g salt

3 g salt

48 g olive oil

16 ounces cremini mushrooms, trimmed and chopped course

2 shallot, minced

1.6 g fresh thyme

52 g dry sherry

14 g minced fresh parsley 7.08 g

10 g sherry vinegar

STEPS

1. bring 4 quarts of water to a boil with 18 g salt and boil farro until tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Drain and keep covered

2. Meanwhile, heat oil in 12 inch skilled over medium high heat until simmering. Add mushrooms, shallots, thyme, and 3 g salt and cook stirring frequently until moisture has evaporated and vegatables start to brown. Stir in sherry and cook scraping up and browned bits until pan is almost dry, 1 to 2 minutes

3. Add farro and cook, stirring constantly, until heated through about 1 minute. Off heat, stir in parsley and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
