1 lbs pasta

12 ounce cherry tomatoes

12 g (.5 oz container) fresh basil leaves

27 g toasted slivered almonds

1 small jarred pepperoncini, stemmed, seeded, and minced

6 g garlic

6 g salt

0.2 g red pepper flake

Process all ingredients except oil until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed
