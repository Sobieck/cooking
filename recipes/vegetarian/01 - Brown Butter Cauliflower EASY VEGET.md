## Traditional, best

Cauliflower Roasted in Brown Butter!

1 cauliflower

12 g canola oil

84 g butter, at room temp and softened

salt

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 450 F 

Cut off stem of cauliflower as close to the base as possible

Remove all leaves.

Rub the cauliflower down with the canola oil

Roast for 45 min

Remove and cover the cauliflower with butter, salt with 3 finger pinch

Roast for another 30 minutes basting a couple times

The cauliflower should be well caramelized and easy to stick with a knife.
