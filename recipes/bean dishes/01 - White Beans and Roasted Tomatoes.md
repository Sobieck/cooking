BEANS

3 cans of cannellini beans 

1 bag frozen pearl onions

TOMATOES

2 large tomatoes, cored and halved crosswise

1 lbs cherry tomatoes

3 g salt

108 g extra virgin olive oil 

GARNISH

8 oz torn fresh basil leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

Cook onions in water, add beans when onions are done

Preheat oven to 500F 

Toss tomatoes in everything but basil. Roast tomatoes until large tomatoes are very tender and have brown patches. 35 to 50 minutes

Server with basil as garnish
