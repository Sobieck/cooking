24 g vegetable oil

1 bag frozen onions

24 g salt

2.3 g pepper

48 g garlic

2 g ground cumin

0.8 g red pepper flake

944 g (4 cups) water

2 14.5 can diced tomatoes

1 bag frozen collard greens

4 15 ounce cans of black eyed peas

30 g cidar vinegar

8 g sugar

## Instructions

1. Heat oil on medium until simmering
2. Add onions and salt. Cook until golden brown about 10 minutes
3. Add garlic, cumin, black pepper, pepper flakes. Cook until fragrent, ~30 seconds
4. Stir in water, tomatoes and juice, bring to a boil.
5. Add greens, reduce heat to low, simmer until just tender ~15 minutes
6. Stir in beans, cover stir occasionally until greens are silky and completely tender. About 15 minutes.
7. Uncover, increase heat to medium high, cook until liquid is nearly evaporated. about 5 minutes. Stir in vinegar and sugar. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
