170g whole wheat flour

9 g baking powder

3 g table salt

25 g granulated sugar

1 large egg

340 g milk, lukewarm

71 g butter


# Instructions


Preheat your iron while you make the waffle batter.

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

In a separate bowl or large measuring cup, whisk together the egg, milk, and butter or oil.

Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring just until combined. The batter will be a bit lumpy; that's OK.

Cook the waffles as directed in the instructions that came with your waffle iron. Serve warm.
