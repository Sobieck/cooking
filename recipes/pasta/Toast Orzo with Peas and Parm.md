28 g butter

1 onion

salt

    4.5 g    

12 g garlic

1 pound orzo

830 g (3.5 cups) water

18 g bouillon

177 g dry white wine

1 pack frozen peas

60 g parmesan

pinch of ground nutmeg

# instructions

1. melt butter over medium
2. Add onion and 4.5 g of salt. Cook until beginning to brown 5 to 7 minutes
3. Add garlic for 30 seconds
4. Add orzo until orzo is slightly toasted and browning, 5 minutes
5. Off heat add water and wine, bring to a boil at medium high
6. Simmer over low for 15 minutes until orzo is tender
7. Stir in peas and parm. Let warm off heat.
