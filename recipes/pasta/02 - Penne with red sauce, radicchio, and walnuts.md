1 lbs penne

1/4 cup olive oil

1 red onion

4 garlic cloves

1 1/2 tablespoons finely chopped anchovies

1 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes 

1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper

1 head radicchio, sliced

1/3 cup chopped parsley

Chopped toasted walnut and shaved pecorina for serving


Cook pasta, reserve 1/3 water

Meanwhile, heat oil, add onion, anchovies, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, cook until softened. 

Add garlic until fragrent

Add crushed tomatoes and red pepper, cook until thickened 10-15 minutes

Stir in pasta and reserved water

Stir in radicchio, cook until wilted

Add parsley

Server with walnuts and cheese
