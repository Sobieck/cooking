1 lbs penne

54 g olive oil

1 red onion

1 1/2 tablespoons finely chopped anchovies 19.5 g

6 g salt

1.15 g black pepper

24 g garlic cloves

1 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes 

0.835 g crushed red pepper

1 head radicchio, sliced

1.87 g chopped parsley

Chopped toasted walnut and shaved pecorina for serving


Cook pasta, reserve 1/3 cup water

Meanwhile, heat oil, add onion, anchovies, salt, black pepper, cook until softened. 

Add garlic until fragrent

Add crushed tomatoes and red pepper, cook until thickened 10-15 minutes

Stir in pasta and reserved water

Stir in radicchio, cook until wilted

Add parsley

Server with walnuts and cheese
