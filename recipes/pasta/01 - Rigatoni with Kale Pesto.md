1 bunch kale

18 g pine nuts, toasted

12 g garlic cloves, chopped

108 g extra virgin olive oil

2 g freshly grated lemon rind 

57 g fresh pecorin romano cheese

3 g kosher salt

1 g freshly ground black pepper

1 lbs rigatoni pasta


INSTRUCTIONS

Boil water, cook kale until deep green, about 15 seconds. Plunge in Ice water

Drain excess liquid

Process kale, pine nuts, and garlic in food processor

Slowly add oil, blend until smooth

Stir in rind, cheese, salt, and pepper

Cook pasta and mix in pesto.
